About Prisma

Your home deserves
precision.

Prisma Windows and Doors Inc. was founded on a simple principle: every installation should be flawless. Based in Ottawa, we bring Canadian craftsmanship to every project — whether you're a homeowner replacing aging windows or a contractor needing reliable installation crews.

Our name reflects our approach. Like light through a prism, we transform raw materials into something beautiful, precise, and lasting. The maple leaf in our logo isn't decoration — it's a promise of quality rooted in Canadian standards.

Full-service experience: we supply premium windows and doors, handle demolition, installation, trim work, sills, and capping. One team, one warranty, zero headaches.

Installation-only service for contractors. You supply the units — we install with precision. Reliable scheduling, clean crews, professional results your clients will love.

Serving Ottawa since day one. Kanata to Orleans, Barrhaven to Gatineau — we know every neighbourhood.
